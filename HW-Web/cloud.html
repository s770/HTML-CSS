Cloudiness


Clouds are visible masses of condensed waste vapor floating in the atmosphere. According to NASA, 67 percent of the Earth’s surface is typically covered by clouds. The data shows three broad/ bands where Earth’s skies are cloudy: a narrow strip near the equator and two wider/ strips in the mid-latitudes.
